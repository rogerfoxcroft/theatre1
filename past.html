Songs for a New Decade

15th December 2025 — Crown Wharf Theatre, Stone

Tickets £15 / £12 (Restricted View)

Join Theatre1 as they reflect on a decade of producing fantastic musical theatre in Staffordshire. After their two wonderful productions in Crown Wharf Theatre, and with plenty more in the pipeline, join this talented group of singers and actors as they bring the best songs from their back catalogue of shows.
