Meet the Founders

The creative team who has shaped Theatre1’s voice across a decade of Off-Broadway and Off-West End inspired productions.

David Reynolds

Director - David is the creative vision behind Theatre1, leading productions from inception to stage with passion, expertise and haze. Lots of haze!

Laura Foxcroft

Musical Director - A graduate of Birmingham Conservatoire Laura brings the musical magic to Theatre1’s performances. Theatre1 is well known for it's strong harmonies and powerful singing.

Natalie Dale

Technical Manager - Natalie crafts the visual and technical world of each show, creating immersive theatrical experiences.

Roger Foxcroft

Secretary - Roger produces Theatre1 shows, ensuring the creative team have all the support they need.
