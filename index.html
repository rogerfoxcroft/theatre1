If/Then

A bold Broadway musical about choice, destiny, and possibility.

Theatre1 proudly presents If/Then — the bold, contemporary Broadway musical rarely seen on local stages. From the Tony Award-winning creators of Next to Normal, If/Then follows Elizabeth as one choice splits her life into two parallel journeys—each filled with love, loss, and possibility. With a stunning, modern score and heartfelt storytelling, this moving production explores how the smallest decisions can shape our destiny.

Known for staging exceptional musicals seldom performed in this region, Theatre1 brings If/Then to life with its signature professionalism, emotional depth, and outstanding local talent. Don’t miss this unforgettable theatrical experience.
