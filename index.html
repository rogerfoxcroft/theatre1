December 2026

A Little Big Christmas

19th December 2026 — Crown Wharf Theatre, Stone

This Christmas, join Theatre1 for A Little Big Christmas — a spectacular evening celebrating the music, magic, and joy of musical theatre at the most wonderful time of the year.

Featuring performances from current and former members of Theatre1 alongside a selection of special guest performers, this festive concert promises an unforgettable night of outstanding live entertainment. Audiences can expect a vibrant mix of songs from hidden gems of the musical theatre world, contemporary crowd favourites, and a collection of beloved Christmas numbers from stage productions through the years.

Filled with festive spirit, powerful performances, and plenty of seasonal cheer, A Little Big Christmas is the perfect way to celebrate the season with friends, family, and a shared love of great music.

July 2027

The Little Big Things

14–17 July 2027 — Crown Wharf Theatre, Stone

The Little Big Things is an uplifting and deeply moving new musical that celebrates the extraordinary strength of the human spirit in the face of life-changing adversity. Inspired by the true story of Henry Fraser, it follows a young man whose world is transformed in an instant, forcing him and those closest to him to confront an uncertain future they never imagined.

At its heart, the show is a powerful exploration of resilience, identity, and the courage it takes to rebuild when life takes an unexpected turn. Through moments of humour, heartbreak, and hope, it shines a light on the importance of inclusion, the challenges of navigating disability, and the determination needed to redefine what is possible.

Above all, The Little Big Things is a story about family, friendship, and discovering that even in our darkest moments, life’s smallest victories can become the biggest triumphs of all.
